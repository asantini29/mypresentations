Extreme mass ratio inspirals

Extreme mass ratio inspirals (EMRIs) represent a prime target for LISA

They will undergo hundreds of thousands of waveform cycles while staying in the LISA band

Unique opurtunity to test fundamental physics and probe the astrophysical environments of MBHs

Four projects

  • Impact of relativistic waveforms in LISA's science objectives with extreme-mass-ratio inspirals
    with Khalvati, Duque, Speri, Gair, Yang, Brito
  • Kerr extension of FastEMRIWaveforms
    with Chapman-Bird+
  • SO3 Figures of merit
    with Mangiagli, Buscicchio; Speri, Chapman-Bird, Kejriwal, Burke
  • EMRI search
    with Gair, Speri, Strub, Duque and the DDPC L2D group
