Impact of relativistic waveforms in LISA's science objectives with extreme-mass-ratio inspirals

We need fast, accurate waveforms

Being long lived sources with a large number of cycles, EMRIs require accurate waveform models to have a small phase error: $\Delta \boldsymbol{\Phi} \sim 1 / \rho$.

Speed is crucial to explore their huge parameter space

Gravitational self-force (GSF)

Kludge models
